What this is

Distinct from the draft overlay. This is pro competitive data, pick-priority, measured traits, and matchups from 1,164 pro games. The live champ-select overlay recommends soloq picks personalized to your lobby; this scouts the pro meta. Pro pick-priority is not soloq strength, read it as "what pros prioritize," not "what climbs."

WR* is winrate shrunk toward 50%. Counter badges list the enemy laners a pick answers into. Trait phrases are the champion's most extreme measured traits vs role peers.
